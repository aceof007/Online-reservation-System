About LuxStay

Redefining luxury hospitality since 2010

Founded in 2010, LuxStay began with a simple vision: to create unforgettable travel experiences by combining luxurious accommodations with personalized service. What started as a single boutique hotel in Paris has grown into a global collection of exceptional properties, each offering a unique sense of place and unparalleled hospitality.

Today, LuxStay operates in over 30 countries, with a portfolio that includes urban retreats, beachfront resorts, and countryside estates. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our founding principles of quality, authenticity, and attention to detail.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality experiences that create lasting memories for our guests, while maintaining the highest standards of service, comfort, and sustainability.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and admired luxury hospitality brand in the world, recognized for our innovative approach to guest experiences and our commitment to excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LuxStay

How do I modify or cancel my reservation?

You can modify or cancel your reservation through our website or mobile app by accessing "My Bookings" in your account. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team at +1 (800) 555-1234 or email support@luxstay.com for assistance.

Please note that cancellation policies vary by property and rate plan. Your specific cancellation terms are provided at the time of booking and in your confirmation email.

What is LuxStay's price match guarantee?

We're confident in offering the best rates for our properties. If you find a lower rate for the same room, dates, and terms on another website within 24 hours of booking with us, we'll match that rate and give you an additional 10% discount.

To request a price match, please contact our customer service team with details of the lower rate you've found.

What amenities can I expect at LuxStay properties?

All LuxStay properties offer premium amenities including:

  • Luxury bedding and bath products
  • High-speed WiFi
  • 24/7 concierge service
  • Fitness centers
  • At least one dining option

Many locations also feature spas, pools, and other unique amenities. Specific amenities for each property are listed on their individual pages.

How does the LuxStay loyalty program work?

Our LuxStay Rewards program offers points for every dollar spent on eligible stays. These points can be redeemed for:

  • Free nights at any LuxStay property
  • Room upgrades
  • Dining and spa credits
  • Exclusive member rates

Members also enjoy benefits like late check-out, welcome amenities, and priority service. Enrollment is free and can be done during booking or through our website.

What health and safety measures are in place?

Guest safety is our top priority. All LuxStay properties adhere to our comprehensive LuxSafe program, which includes:

  • Enhanced cleaning protocols
  • Contactless check-in/check-out options
  • Hand sanitizer stations throughout properties
  • Regular staff health screenings
  • Hospital-grade air filtration systems

Specific measures may vary by location based on local guidelines.

Do you offer special rates for group travel?

Yes, we offer special rates and amenities for groups of 10 rooms or more. Our Group Travel specialists can help with:

  • Customized room blocks
  • Meeting and event space
  • Catering arrangements
  • Transportation coordination
  • Special group activities

Contact our Group Travel team at groups@luxstay.com or +1 (800) 555-5678 for personalized assistance.
