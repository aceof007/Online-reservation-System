About Grand Hotel Paris

Located in the heart of Paris, just steps from the Opéra Garnier and the famous Rue de la Paix shopping district, Grand Hotel Paris is a legendary 5-star palace that has been welcoming guests since 1862. This architectural masterpiece combines Haussmannian elegance with contemporary luxury.

Our hotel features 180 rooms and suites, each uniquely decorated with period furnishings and modern amenities. The property boasts three gourmet restaurants, including a Michelin-starred dining experience, an expansive spa with indoor pool, and exceptional personalized service that has made us a favorite among discerning travelers for generations.

The hotel's historic facade and ornate interiors have been meticulously preserved, while discreetly incorporating all the modern comforts expected by today's luxury travelers. From the moment you step into our marble-floored lobby with its spectacular chandelier, you'll understand why Grand Hotel Paris remains one of the city's most iconic addresses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and possibly an additional charge.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Breakfast is included in some room rates (like our Executive Suite) but not in others. You can see whether breakfast is included when selecting your room type. Breakfast can also be added to your reservation for an additional fee.

Does the hotel have parking?

Yes, we offer valet parking service for €50 per night. Due to our central Paris location, self-parking is not available. There is also a public parking garage nearby at Place Vendôme.

How far is the hotel from the Eiffel Tower?

The Eiffel Tower is approximately 3.5 km (2.2 miles) from our hotel. It's about a 20-minute drive or you can take Metro line 8 from Opéra to École Militaire, which takes about 25 minutes total.

Does the hotel offer airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange private airport transfers in luxury vehicles. The cost is €120 from Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) and €90 from Orly Airport (ORY). Please contact our concierge at least 48 hours before your arrival to arrange this service.
